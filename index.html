Day 1
The request

Three photos of a cluttered living room and a short brief: "light, warm, and the sofa needs to fit next to the dining area." Budget: up to €4,000.

Day 1
AI concepts arrive

In 12 minutes: 5 concepts across Scandinavian, Japandi and minimalist styles. Japandi wins — warm wood, soft light.

Day 2–3
A designer takes over

Layout checked against real measurements. Two pieces swapped for in-stock alternatives from local suppliers. Budget recalculated to the euro.

Day 4
Final handover

6 final visualizations, a scaled floor plan, and a materials list with supplier links.
